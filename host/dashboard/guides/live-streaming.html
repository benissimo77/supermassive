Admins: Live-streaming Guide

This guide is for administrators who want to live-stream games to a wider audience.

  • OBS Setup: Use OBS or similar software to capture the Host Scene window.
  • Audio: Ensure the game audio is being captured correctly in your stream.
  • Latency: Be aware of stream latency. You may need to adjust the question timers to give remote players enough time to respond.
  • Moderation: Keep an eye on the player list and chat to ensure a positive experience for everyone.
